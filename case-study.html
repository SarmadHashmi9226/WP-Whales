Subscription Groups

BuddyBoss’s native functionality didn’t support paid groups, a critical requirement for Athletes Ocean. Instructors needed a way to monetize group memberships seamlessly.

Solution

  • Added the ability for instructors to create paid or free groups directly from the BuddyBoss group creation process.

  • Automated the backend process to generate a subscription product for paid groups, with pricing defined by instructors.

  • Linked the subscription product with the group and added a Buy Now button for paid groups in the group directory.

  • Developed logic to handle subscription failures, automatically removing group access for unpaid students.

Impact

Simplified the monetization process for instructors, leading to a significant increase in group memberships and student engagement.

HLS Streaming Transition

The platform relied on outdated video streaming methods that were inefficient for varying network conditions, leading to poor user experiences.

Solution

  • Developed logic to handle subscription failures, automatically removing group access for unpaid students. Transitioned the video streaming system to HLS (HTTP Live Streaming) for optimized delivery.

  • Integrated Bunny Stream to handle video storage and HLS delivery.

  • Enhanced compatibility for mobile and desktop users with smoother playback.

Impact

Improved video delivery quality and engagement, keeping students on the platform longer and boosting course completion rates.

Video Upload Enhancement

Instructors faced long wait times (up to 30 minutes) for uploading large training videos, disrupting their workflows.

Solution

  • Developed a custom React-based video uploader with TUS upload protocol.

  • Enabled background uploads, allowing instructors to continue using the platform while videos are processed.

  • Notifications were introduced to inform instructors when their videos were live.

Impact

Reduced upload times dramatically and enhanced instructor satisfaction, ensuring they could focus on creating content rather than managing technical issues

UI/UX Transformation

The platform’s homepage and sales directories were outdated, with slow filtering and pagination navigation that disrupted user journeys.

Solution

  • Redesigned the homepage with a slider-based layout showcasing sections like Free Content, Featured Courses, Best Sellers, and Top Subscription Groups.

  • Replaced pagination with infinite scrolling for better navigation on sales directories.

  • Converted filters to Alpine.js, providing real-time filtering without page reloads.

Impact

  • 215% increase in monthly revenue.

  • Improved user engagement and seamless browsing, resulting in higher conversion rates.

Instructors Commission Model

The platform’s payment system didn’t allow control over retention periods or instructor withdrawals, causing operational issues.

Solution

  • Built a custom Stripe Connect solution tailored for Athletes Ocean.

  • Allowed the platform to set a retention period for funds before instructors could withdraw, minimizing refund disputes.

Impact

Streamlined the payment process for instructors, improving trust and reliability between instructors and the platform.

Team Space Development

Gym owners wanted to enroll multiple members under a single subscription, but the platform lacked functionality to handle team-based purchases.

Solution

  • Developed Team Spaces, allowing gym owners to purchase bulk seats for their team members.

  • Built logic to differentiate pricing for individual users versus gym owners.

  • Automated course and group access for all team members once purchased.

Impact

Enabled scalable group subscriptions, increasing sales from gyms and improving team collaboration on the platform.
